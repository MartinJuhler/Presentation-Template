Why It Cannot Work

The Core Problems

Even with additional investment, Infor CPQ is structurally incapable of meeting AKVA's needs.

Existing Setup — Status Quo
🔄
No Automatic Data Sync
Prices come via FnOCOLL, but product data, items, and options are 100% static. Any ERP change breaks CPQ silently. Manual upkeep for tens of thousands of components.
📂
Convoluted Interface
Myriad of interdependent matrices. Updates don't propagate. UI so convoluted that participants were highly confused. Requires coding knowledge to operate.
👥
One Developer at a Time
No branching, no merging, no automated testing. Changes traced via manual comments only. Doesn't scale.
💥
No Safety Logic
No protection against cascading failures. Renamed or removed items produce wrong quotes or crashes — with zero warning.
Proposed New Approach — Still Broken
🤷
Infor Can't Commit
Support is siloed. No single person understands the full flow. Their best proposal has no production references, no timeline, no cost.
🔮
Only Standard Products
Even their proposed new approach only covers standard items. Complex products like flåte are not solvable in this architecture.
