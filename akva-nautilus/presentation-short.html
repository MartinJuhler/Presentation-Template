THE CHALLENGE

Sea lice cost billions annually. Treatments stress fish and resistance is growing.

🦠

The Sea Lice Crisis

Chemical and mechanical treatments are increasingly ineffective as parasites develop resistance. Each treatment cycle costs €50K–€100K per site.

🌊

Nature's Answer

Sea lice concentrate in the top 10–15m of water. By farming below 20m, fish grow in a virtually lice-free environment — no chemicals needed.

💨

Air Dome Innovation

Patented air dome at depth lets salmon refill swim bladders naturally. Waterborne feeding and AI cameras ensure optimal conditions 24/7.
