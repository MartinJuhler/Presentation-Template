The Challenge

Sea lice (Lepeophtheirus salmonis) remain the single largest biological challenge in salmon farming, costing the industry billions annually in treatments, lost growth, and fish welfare issues. Traditional chemical and mechanical delousing operations are stressful for fish and increasingly ineffective as resistance develops.

The Solution

🌊 Submersible Pens

Nautilus lowers the net and fish below 20m depth — beneath the sea lice zone — while the buoyant pen stays on the surface.

💨 Air Dome Technology

Patented air dome at depth ensures salmon can refill swim bladders daily, maintaining welfare and natural behavior underwater.

🎯 Precision Feeding

Waterborne feeding delivers feed directly to the submerged biomass. AI-assisted cameras monitor appetite and optimize feeding in real time.

📊 AI Monitoring

AKVA smarteye and submerged cameras track biomass, weight distribution, lice counts, and health parameters 24/7 at depth.
