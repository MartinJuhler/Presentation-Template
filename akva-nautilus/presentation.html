HOW IT WORKS

Nautilus: engineered for life at depth

01

Submersible Net

Buoyant pen stays on surface. Net with integrated roof and air dome lowers to >20m via straps under the pen. Standard frame mooring, NS 9415:21 certified.

02

Air Dome Technology

Patented dome at the net roof center provides constant air access. Salmonids refill swim bladders daily — the dome ensures natural behavior at depth.

03

Waterborne Feeding

Feed delivered directly through the water column to submerged biomass. Energy-efficient and precise, reaching fish exactly where they are.

04

AI Monitoring

AKVA smarteye modular cameras + AKVA submerged tracking: biomass estimation, lice counting, weight distribution, and health parameters — all at depth, 24/7.
